- Truth. Goodness. Beauty. -
Life is a journey along which we must choose a path, the City of God or the City of Man,
the road towards Heaven or the road towards Hell. In The Lord of the Rings, we see this dichotomy through
the interplay between characters on disparate roads. Will we be Aragorn or Isildur, Gandalf or Sarumon,
Frodo or Gollum? Will we pursue lives of virtue and moral character or will we be motivated by power,
selfish ambition, and conceit? The choice is before each of us. The Gamgee Conversations are inspired
by the great literary character Samwise Gamgee from The Lord of the Rings. Samwise is humble, loyal,
perseverent, and faithful. We must take on the role of Frodo, a willingness to say, “I will take the ring
though I do not know the way.” Our pursuit of the true, the good, and the beautiful is a perilous road filled
with temptations to use these ends in themselves as perverted means to corrupt ends. God has created us
as communal creatures for a reason. We all need Samwise Gamgees to accompany us on our journeys.
Through these conversations, we will take on the task of Frodo and play the role of Samwise for those
who journey with us. Together we can find the world of answers.
